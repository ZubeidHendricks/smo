Welcome to the Health Service Provider Platform

This platform is an innovative way that introduces efficiencies in how we onboard NPO, how NPO's create and submit work plans for review and approval and how we monitor and evaluate the services NPO's provide. In addition this platform will allow for broader and efficient engagements around contracts and services.
